STAY SAFE FROM CORONAVIRUS

As you know, Coronavirus has now been spread all over the world. To protect overselves from this disease, following steps can be taken:

1.Wash your hands frequently

You need to wash your hands thoroughly and frequently using an alcohol-based hand rub or wash them with soap and water.

2.Avoid touching eyes, nose or mouth

If you have touched some places which may contain virus, it is important that the virus can spread when you touch your eyes, nose or mouth. So, touching these parts after washing is only applicable.

3.Maintain a minimum distance

You need to maintain a minimum of 1 metre distance between you and the person who is sneezing/coughing.

4.Maintain respiratory hygiene

You must cover your mouth as well as nose with tissue while sneezing/coughing. If you don't have a tissue, you must use your elbow to cover your mouth and nose.

Consult doctor at the earliest

If you have cough, fever, sneezing or facing breathing trouble, you must visit the nearest hospital or call the Corona helpline number.

Follow the advices

You must strictly follow the advice given by the health officer. Also take into notice that it is not only you who get affected if you do not follow the advices, instead it affect your surrounding persons too.
